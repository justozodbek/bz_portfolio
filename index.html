Minimal Architecture

This architectural study explores the relationship between space and light in a minimalist environment. The clean lines and subtle textures create a sense of calm and order, while the carefully placed lighting adds depth and warmth.

The project was created using Blender and rendered with Cycles, focusing on achieving realistic yet soft lighting conditions that enhance the spatial qualities of the design.
